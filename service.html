Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try before I buy?

Absolutely! We offer a free facility tour and one complimentary group class for all new visitors. Personal training clients get 20% off their first session.

Are there any contracts?

We offer both month-to-month and discounted annual options. Month-to-month memberships can be cancelled with 30 days notice. No hidden fees or cancellation charges.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my membership?

Yes! You can change your membership level at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle.
