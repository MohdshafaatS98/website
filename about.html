About FitPro

Meet the team dedicated to your fitness success

Our Mission

At FitPro, we believe fitness is more than just physical transformation. It's about building confidence, developing discipline, and creating a lifestyle that supports your overall wellbeing. Founded in 2015, we've helped over 5,000 clients achieve their fitness goals through personalized training, expert nutrition guidance, and unwavering support.

Our state-of-the-art facility features the latest equipment, spacious workout areas, and a welcoming community atmosphere that motivates you to push beyond your limits.

Our Story

FitPro was born from a simple vision: to create a fitness space where everyone feels welcome, supported, and empowered to reach their goals. What started as a small 2,000 sq ft gym in 2015 has grown into a thriving 10,000 sq ft fitness center serving over 500 active members. Our success isn't measured in square footage, but in the lives we've transformed—from helping new moms regain their strength to training athletes for competition, and everything in between.

Our Fitness Philosophy

We believe in a holistic approach to fitness that combines strength training, cardiovascular health, proper nutrition, and mental wellness. Every client is unique, and we design programs that respect your individual goals, limitations, and lifestyle. Our trainers focus on sustainable habits rather than quick fixes, ensuring long-term success and health.

Meet Our Expert Trainers

Head Trainer - Sarah Johnson

12 Years Experience | Specialization: Personal Training & Sports Nutrition

With 12 years of experience in personal training and sports nutrition, Sarah has helped hundreds of clients transform their bodies and lives. Her holistic approach combines strength training, cardiovascular conditioning, and mindful eating practices. Sarah believes in empowering clients with knowledge, teaching them not just what to do, but why they're doing it. Her passion for fitness began as a competitive swimmer, and she brings that same dedication to every client she works with.

Training Philosophy: "Fitness isn't about perfection—it's about progress. I help my clients build sustainable habits that fit their lifestyle, not someone else's ideal."

Certifications & Qualifications:

NASM Certified Personal Trainer Precision Nutrition Level 2 CrossFit Level 2 Trainer TRX Certified Instructor Sports Nutrition Specialist First Aid & CPR Certified

Strength Coach - Mike Chen

10 Years Experience | Specialization: Strength & Conditioning

Mike specializes in strength and conditioning, with a background in competitive powerlifting. He's passionate about helping clients build functional strength that translates to everyday activities and athletic performance. Mike has coached everyone from complete beginners to competitive athletes, and he excels at breaking down complex movements into simple, achievable steps. His patient teaching style and focus on proper form have helped countless clients avoid injury while achieving impressive strength gains.

Training Philosophy: "Strength training isn't just about lifting heavy weights—it's about moving well, building resilience, and feeling powerful in your daily life."

Certifications & Qualifications:

CSCS - Certified Strength & Conditioning Specialist USA Weightlifting Level 1 FMS - Functional Movement Screen Precision Nutrition Level 1 Powerlifting Coach Certification First Aid & CPR Certified
